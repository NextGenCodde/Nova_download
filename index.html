Nova - AI Voice Assistant

Nova is a powerful desktop AI assistant built with Python. Use your voice to open apps, play music, get weather updates, search online, and chat with an AI model like ChatGPT.

🎙️ Voice Activation - Just say "Nova"
🌐 Opens websites & apps via voice
🎵 Play local music from your library
🔍 Google & YouTube voice search
📆 Get current date, time & weather
💬 Chat with AI using DeepSeek API
🛑 Say "stop" to deactivate Nova
